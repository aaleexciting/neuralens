Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 5, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Neuralens ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you access or use our AI detection service (the "Service").

Information We Collect

  • Uploaded Images & URLs: When you upload an image or submit a URL, we process this data to provide the analysis. We do not store your images permanently. They are sent to a third-party API (Sightengine) for analysis and are subject to their data policies. A temporary, compressed thumbnail may be stored for your session history.
  • Anonymous User ID: To provide the "Analysis History" feature, we use Firebase Authentication to assign you a temporary, anonymous user ID. This ID is not linked to any personal information like your name, email, or IP address.
  • Analysis History: We store the results of your analyses (e.g., probability score, status, source type, and timestamp) linked to your anonymous user ID. This data is stored in our Firebase Firestore database.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • Display your recent analysis history for your convenience during your session.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.

Data Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties, except as follows:

  • With Service Providers: We share image data with Sightengine to perform the AI detection analysis.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if we believe that disclosure is in accordance with, or required by, any applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your analysis history is stored to enhance your user experience. You have the ability to clear your entire history at any time using the "Clear All" button on the main page. This action permanently deletes all analysis records associated with your anonymous ID from our database.

Changes to this Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy.
